FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is HachStacks about?

    HachStacks provides a platform and a searchlight for identifying tech-oriented minds with burning passion in learning more, nurture them by providing resources necessary for their growth, encourage and mentor through regular meetups and events, access to carefully selected patrons and resource persons.

  • What are the benefits of joining HachStacks Community?

    1. Access to premium resources.
    2. 24/7 access to community mentoring.
    3. Real time solutions to problems.
    4. Job offers and opportunities.

  • Can i donate to the community or sponsor an event?

    Yes, We absolutely love that. To donate to the community or sponsor any of our event, please send a DM to any of our social media handles.

  • How can i join the Community?

    1. Go to "Join Us" page and fill the form
    2. Like and follow all the community online platforms

  • Is HachStacks limited to Akwa Ibom State University students?

    No! HachStacks is open to membership to all interested individuals.

  • I dont have knowledge of any digital skill, can i join?

    Yes, you can but you must have a laptop and other necessary tools for learning in your chosen field.

  • I am a member of another Tech Community, Can i still join?

    Yes, you can.

  • Will there be physical meetups?

    Yes, there will be physical meetups.

  • I have more questions! Whom do i contact?

    Go to "Contact Us" page and send a DM to us. Alternatively, send a DM to any of our Social media handles.
